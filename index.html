Who we are

Good Vibes, Great Songs

Miss Chief and the Bandits are a six-piece pop rock cover band with one simple mission: fill your venue, your dance floor, and your soul with nothing but pure, unfiltered fun.

From anthems you screamed in your car to guilty pleasures you secretly adore, we play it all with a smile and a whole lot of energy. No pretension, no slow moments — just great music and even better vibes.

Perfect for festivals, corporate parties, club nights, or any occasion that deserves an upgrade from a Spotify playlist.

★ The Miss Chiefs ★

Nikki

Vocals

The voice that lights up the room before she even sings a note. Nikki brings heart, sass, and unstoppable stage presence to every show.

Emma

Vocals

Power, sweetness, and a smile that could melt stadiums. Together with Nikki, Emma makes every chorus twice as dangerous.

⚡ The Bandits ⚡

Ruud

Guitar

Riffs that'll make your hair stand on end and solos that'll make you forget your own name. Ruud keeps the fire burning six strings at a time.

Erik-Jan

Bass

The groove machine. Erik-Jan lays down the low end with surgical precision and a coolness that makes the whole band look good.

Pieter

Keyboards

From lush synth pads to thundering piano chords, Pieter fills every corner of the room with sound and leaves no ear unattended.

Ingo

Drums & Vocals

The heartbeat of the Bandits. Ingo drives the engine hard, keeps everyone in line, and still somehow finds time to sing about it.

🚗💨 The Accomplices 🚗💨

David

Chief of Sound

The man behind the mix. While the band steals songs, David steals the show from behind the desk — shaping every note, balancing every voice, and making sure the kick drum hits you right in the chest. The unsung hero of every great night out.

🎤 Sound

The band carries its own IEM rig with passive splitters (Behringer Ultralink MS8000) and a Behringer Wing Rack. All members use wired in-ears with individual headphone amplifiers.

Lead vocals rotate per song between the two vocalists and drummer.

⚡ Venue provides

  • Drum mics & cabling (kick, snare, 3× rack tom, floor tom, 2× overhead)
  • 3× vocal mic (SM58 or similar) + stands
  • 3 power outlets (230V) for amps
  • Mixing desk
  • PA system
  • 🍬 M&Ms — blue ones only. We're serious.

The band can also offer to bring a PA system. Please contact us on the possibilities.

📦 Band brings

  • Drum kit
  • Bass pre-amp / DI
  • Guitar pre-amp / DI
  • Keys / DI
  • IEM rig (Behringer Wing Rack + Ultralink)
  • In-ears + headphone amplifiers
  • 🐄🔔 More cowbell
